## 📅 Week by Week Breakdown

---

### ✅ Week 1 — Data Cleaning & Preparation (Excel)

**Objective:** Prepare the raw E-Commerce dataset for analysis by resolving data quality issues.

**Tasks Performed:**
- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing and null values
- Standardised column headers and data types
- Formatted date columns correctly for time-series analysis
- Saved a clean, analysis-ready version of the dataset

**Outcome:** A clean, consistent dataset ready for exploration and analysis ✅

---

### ✅ Week 2 — Exploratory Data Analysis & Basic Statistics (Excel)

**Objective:** Explore the cleaned dataset to identify patterns, trends and distributions.

**Tasks Performed:**
- Calculated descriptive statistics (mean, median, min, max, standard deviation)
- Analysed sales distribution across products, referral sources and payment methods
- Explored monthly sales trends to identify seasonal patterns
- Summarised order status distribution (Delivered, Shipped, Pending, Returned, Cancelled)
- Used Pivot Tables to aggregate and cross-tabulate key metrics


**Key Findings:**
- Chair and Printer are the top revenue-generating products at **$196K each**
- Instagram is the highest revenue referral source at **$0.28M**
- June recorded the peak monthly sales at **$171K**
- Gift Card and Online are the most used payment methods

### ✅ Week 4 — Data Visualization & Storytelling (Power BI)

**Objective:** Build an interactive Power BI dashboard to communicate insights clearly.

**Dashboard Features:**
- **KPI Cards** — Total Sales, Quantities, Customers, AOV and Orders at a glance
- **Revenue by Product** — Horizontal bar chart showing top products by revenue
- **Orders by Payment Method** — Donut chart showing payment method distribution
- **Revenue by Referral Source** — Bar chart comparing Instagram, Email, Google, Facebook and Referral
- **Monthly Sales Trend** — Line chart revealing seasonal sales patterns across the year
- **Order Status Distribution** — Bar chart showing Cancelled, Returned, Pending, Shipped and Delivered orders
- **Interactive Slicers** — Filter by Coupon Code, Year and Order Status

**Key Insights from Dashboard:**
- Total revenue reached **$1.26M** with **1K orders** from **1K unique customers**
- **June** was the best performing month with **$171K** in sales
- Sales dropped significantly in **July ($86K)** before recovering toward year end
- **Cancelled orders (250)** are the highest order status — a key area for improvement
- **Instagram** drives the most revenue among all referral sources at **$0.28M**
- Payment methods are **evenly distributed** across all five categories

## 💡 Overall Project Insights

The E-Commerce business generated **$1.26M in total sales** with an average order value of **$1.05K**. Sales peak mid-year in June before dropping sharply, suggesting a need for targeted promotions in the second half of the year. Instagram is the strongest acquisition channel, and Chair and Printer are the best-selling products. However, the high rate of **Cancelled (250) and Returned (247) orders** signals potential issues with customer satisfaction or product quality that need urgent attention.

> **Recommendation:** Focus marketing spend on Instagram, investigate reasons for high cancellations and returns, and introduce mid-year promotions to sustain June's peak performance through Q3 and Q4.
